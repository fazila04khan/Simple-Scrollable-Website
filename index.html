What is edventure park?

EdVenture Park is a student-focused idea stage startup incubator which aims to empower student led startups through an intensive 4 month pre Incubation programme.
Students can experiment with their ideas and have an opportunity of starting their own company while still in college.

Who are campus leads?

CEO of edventure park at your respective college. Bridge between edventure park and other people.

Here are some frequently asked questions!


  1. I am not familiar with coding, can i still apply?
    Yes, You can. You don't need any prior coding skills to proceed with the idea. You will learn it eventually in the process if you wish or We provide you with technical mentors:)


  2. What is a cohort?
    In simple words, cohort means batch of people ie. a group. In each cohort, We select 25 startups and help them take their idea to next level.


  3. Can a Graduate/non-student apply?
    Why not? We are student-focused, not student-only.
